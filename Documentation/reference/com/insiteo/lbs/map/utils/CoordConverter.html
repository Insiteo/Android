Class Overview

Converts coordinates for 3 systems of references : - screen coordinates (in pixel, with (0,0) at the top left corner of the displayed surface) - real coordinates (in meter, with (0,0) at the top left corner of the map) - map coordinates (in pixel at zoom level 0, with (0,0) at the top left corner of the map)
